About Project D

Founded by the visionary strategist Ryosuke Takahashi, Project D is a specialized, high-efficiency racing team built for a singular purpose: to crush every local course record across the Kanto region. Operating on a strict one-year timeline, we do not race for fame or money—we race to perfect the art of street racing and leave behind an unbeatable legend.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an unparalleled racing experience for both our drivers and fans. We strive to foster a community that celebrates the thrill of racing while promoting safety, sportsmanship, and technological advancement.

Racing Background

Our formidable dual-ace system splits the touge into two absolute disciplines. Downhill specialist Takumi Fujiwara commands the legendary, lightweight Toyota AE86, defying physics with unparalleled instinct and adaptive drift techniques. Uphill specialist Keisuke Takahashi pilots the high-horsepower Mazda RX-7 FD3S, mastering technical precision and raw power acceleration. Backed by a dedicated support crew and Ryosuke’s data-driven simulations, Project D turns racing into an exact science.
