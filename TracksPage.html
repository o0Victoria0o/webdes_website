PROJECT D Tracks and Touges Tackled

"Whether or Downhill or Uphill, We've Set the Standards"

Mount Akina

(秋名山 Akina-san) The course famous for it's five consecutive hairpins.

  • Length: 6.8 km (4.2 mi)
  • Elevation: 1,200 m (3,900 ft)
  • Number of Turns: 48
  • Downhill Record: 4:30.31
  • Uphill Record: 4:58.20

Irohazaka

(いろは坂, literally Iroha Slope) has a rhythm of straight-hairpin-straight-hairpin-straight

  • Length: 4.3 km (2.7 mi)
  • Elevation: 893 m (2,930 ft)
  • Number of Turns: 48
  • Downhill Record: 3:56.91
  • Uphill Record: 4:21.12

  • Uphill Record: 4:39.78
